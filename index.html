Society for Social Welfare

PERCEPTIONS, registered in June 2010, is a Bhopal based non-government organization, promoted by a group of Bhopal based artists & academicians inclined towards promotion of indigenous tribal and folk arts and crafts. Perceptions work to support Rural and Tribal artists and their overall development issues like Education, Health and Environment; following are the objectives-

  • * To search talents of Tribal or Rural Arts,
  • * Providing them technical guidance,
  • * Exhibiting their work and
  • * Enhancing sales and marketing of their products

Key Activities: Support & Encourage Tribal and Rural Artists for betterment of their livelihoods and to improve their socio-economic family status (Health, Education and environment)

The Organization & its Work:
Frequent training programs on latest trends and techniques of various arts, many workshops were being organized for tribal & rural artists as well as for school and college students. Perceptions, focuses in Tribal Districts of Madhya Pradesh.

Roadmap

Strategy / Approach:

  • Stage 1:

    Action research for identification of need, potentials and problems in all three identified areas of work; with the help of research findings.

  • Stage 2:

    Preparations of action plans, fund raising, uses of self generated funds of Perceptions.

  • Stage 3:

    Actually working in the field for the aimed project.
